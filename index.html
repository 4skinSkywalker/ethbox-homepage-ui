«Well... What if I did something wrong?»

Simply cancel

Go to the History tab, locate and cancel your transaction and get your money back.

«How does the recipient get the funds?»

Just accept

The recipient of your choice has to connect his wallet, go to the Receive tab and accept the transaction you've sent.

Exchange tokens with people

Trade ETH or any ERC-20 token peer-to-peer, trustlessly.

Using the smart contract’s features, over-the-counter (OTC) trading is vastly simplified, as the transfer of funds can only be completed when all terms are satisfied.

Funds can be sent & requested in a single transaction, which may only be fulfilled once both ends have put their part.

This gives users a place which is immune to fraud or scam.

Anonymity

Ethbox sets itself apart by concealing blockchain transactions, making the sender and receiver untraceable.

Our token

EBOX is our utility & governance token, is used to fuel the system (utility) and manage developments (governance).

Deflationary

Smart contract logic makes sure that 25% of ethbox's generated fee are used to buy back & burn EBOX tokens. All EBOX holders profit from the shrinking token supply.

A live view will be available in the near future.

Utility

Reduced service cost / fee for all ethbox services by holding EBOX token.

For transactions below $300, ethbox is free to use. For more details consult our Whitepaper.

Governance

Standing true to our roots within the community, EBOX holders can vote on key subjects regarding the development of ethbox. Anyone holding EBOX tokens is eligible to participate free of extra charge.

A live view will be available in the near future.

Staking

To support those who support us, ethbox offers staking rewards for the first 4 years after the token generation event (TGE) for everyone holding EBOX tokens.

Payout

As ethbox is a project by the community, for the community, all users will benefit from the success of ethbox. We are regularly sharing part of the generated ethbox service fee to all EBOX holders.
